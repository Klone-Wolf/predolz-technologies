Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the price?

All packages include design, development, mobile responsiveness, basic SEO, contact form integration, and the specified number of revision rounds. You'll also receive training on how to update your content.

How long does it take to build a website?

Timeline varies by package: landing pages take about 1 week, one-page websites take 1-2 weeks, and standard multi-page websites take 2-3 weeks. Custom projects may take longer depending on complexity.

Do I need to provide content and images?

Yes, you'll need to provide your text content, logo, and any specific images you want to use. We can help guide you on what content is needed and recommend stock photos if you don't have professional images.

What if I need more pages later?

No problem! We can add additional pages to your website at any time. We'll provide a quote based on the complexity and content of the new pages you need.

Do you offer hosting and domain services?

We can help you set up hosting and register a domain, or work with your existing hosting provider. Hosting and domain costs are separate from our design and development fees.
